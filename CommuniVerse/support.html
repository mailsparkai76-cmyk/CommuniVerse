Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?
You can sign up using your email and password or continue with Google login.
2. How do I update my profile?
Go to Settings from the menu and update your name or profile photo.
3. Can I change my username?
No, usernames are unique and cannot be changed once created.
4. I forgot my password. What should I do?
Use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page to reset it.
5. How do I send messages or make calls?
Select a user from your chat list and start messaging or use the call buttons for voice/video calls.
6. How do I report a problem?
You can contact us via email or Instagram.
